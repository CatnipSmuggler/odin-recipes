Lasagna

Description

Italian dish consiting of long plate noodles with meat and cheese stacked on one another to make layers. It will take a couple of hours to bake.

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound bulk Italion sausage
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1 cup sliced black olives
  • 1 pinch basil
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 TBSP dried oregano
  • 1 1/2 tsp dried sage
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 TBSP minced garlic
  • 1 TBSP dried onion flakes
  • 2(14 oz) jars marinara sauce
  • 1(16 oz) package lasagna noodles
  • 8 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 pound mozzarella cheese, shredded
